Pasta Carbonara

Description

Pasta carbonara is a classic Italian dish that is simple to make but packed with flavor. It is a perfect dish for a quick and easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 pound pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

Step by step guide

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the pancetta or guanciale and cook until crisp.
  3. Remove the pancetta or guanciale from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the bowl with the egg mixture.
  6. Toss to coat the spaghetti in the egg mixture.
  7. Add the pancetta or guanciale back to the skillet and heat through.
  8. Pour the spaghetti and egg mixture into the skillet with the pancetta or guanciale.
  9. Toss to combine and serve immediately.

Arepas

Description

Arepas can be served for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. They are also a popular snack food. So next time you're looking for a delicious and easy-to-make meal, try arepas!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pre-cooked white cornmeal (such as P.A.N.®)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups warm water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil, for frying (optional)

Step by step guide

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the cornmeal and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in the warm water until a soft, dough forms.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces.
  5. Roll each piece of dough into a ball, then flatten it into a 4-inch circle.
  6. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  7. Cook each arepa for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Serve the arepas warm with your favorite fillings, such as cheese, shredded chicken, or avocado.

Lasagna

Description

A small lasagna can be a great option for a quick and easy weeknight meal, or for a special occasion when you want to enjoy all the flavors of lasagna without the leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 6 lasagna noodles
  • 1 cup of meat sauce
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of black pepper

Step by step guide

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9x5 inch baking dish.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of the baking dish.
  5. Top with 3 lasagna noodles.
  6. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  7. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  8. Top with another 3 lasagna noodles.
  9. Spread the remaining meat sauce over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
